Heart Disease Information

Heart disease encompasses various conditions affecting the heart and blood vessels. It's a leading cause of death worldwide, with risk factors including high blood pressure, cholesterol, smoking, and obesity. Prevention involves maintaining a healthy lifestyle through diet, exercise, and avoiding tobacco. Medical treatments and regular check-ups play a crucial role in managing heart health and reducing the risk of complications.

For more information, you can visit American Heart Association.
